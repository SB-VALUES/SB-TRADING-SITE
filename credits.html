About

SB-VALUES is a fan-made site created to make trading a more user-friendly experience, and make sure you always get the best possible deals. The site is updated often and is well taken care of. We take community suggestions and are completely free to use without ads. Our values are trustable and made sure to be as accurate as possible. For any further information please contact @catlover591723 (developer), or @oniscool (value specialist).

How To Use & Basic Info

SB-VALUES is made to compare trades and get a good trade. Hit effects have different values which can effect how good your trade is. Values can fluctuate due to demand and other reasons. To start checking your trade click check-trade on the home-page, or scroll below trade checker. On the left click add for the hit effects you're offering, on the right click add for the hit effects the other person is offering. Once you are done adding to the trade, your total value and the other person's total value will be compared, and in the middle line there will be a box that says "Win", "Fair", or "Lose". A win means you are getting the better trade and you should do the trade! Fair means both values are equal and the trade is even. And lose means you are getting a bad trade, and you shouldn't do it.
